Overview

The water cycle describes the continuous movement of water on, above, and below the surface of the Earth. Water can change states among liquid, vapor, and ice at various places in the water cycle.

Evaporation

Evaporation is the process by which water changes from a liquid to a gas or vapor. This is a key step in the water cycle, driven by the suns energy.

Condensation

Condensation is the process by which water changes from a liquid to a gas or vapor.

Precipitation

Precipitation is the process by which gas chnages from a gas to a liquid

Collection

Collection describes the process of accumulating water from precipitation. Water collects in places like rivers, lakes etc...
